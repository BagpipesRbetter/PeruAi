About Peru

Peru functions as a dedicated web client that interfaces with Meta's cutting-edge Llama2 model, which stands out with an expansive parameter count of 7 billion. Notably, Peru offers users access to a reservoir of up-to-date data that surpasses even the information available to OpenAi's ChatGPT4. By acting as this interface, Peru empowers users to seamlessly engage with the Llama2 model. Peru was created by Evan Barclay in 2023 last update (Tue Aug 8)
